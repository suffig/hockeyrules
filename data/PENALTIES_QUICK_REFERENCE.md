## üìã Special Rules You Need to Know

### ‚ö†Ô∏è SHORT-HANDED Definition
- Team is **BELOW numerical strength** when goal scored
- **Releases:** Minor, Bench Minor (automatically on goal)
- **Does NOT release:** Major penalties
- **Note:** Misconduct alone doesn't make team short-handed

### ‚ö†Ô∏è AUTOMATIC GAME MISCONDUCT
Triggers automatic ejection:
1. **Second Major** in same game
2. **Second Misconduct** in same game  
3. **Specific Infractions:** See Table 6 (Major+Auto GM infractions)

### ‚ö†Ô∏è COINCIDENTAL PENALTIES
- **Equal penalties** = Both sit, no substitution, released at next stoppage
- **Multiple penalties** = Cancel majors first, then minors
- **Last 5 minutes:** Minor vs Major = 3-min differential as major

### ‚ö†Ô∏è DELAYED PENALTY
- Ref raises arm when non-puck team offends
- Play continues
- Penalty assessed when offending team gets puck
- Does NOT apply to coincidental penalties

### ‚ö†Ô∏è PENALTY SHOT VIOLATIONS
| Violation | Consequence |
|---|---|
| 1st (leaving crease, etc.) | Shot retaken |
| 2nd (after failure) | Misconduct + retake |
| 3rd | Game Misconduct + Goal awarded |

## üß† Key Concepts

### Fighting Penalties (Rule 46)
- **Unwilling to fight:** Major (no auto GM)
- **Willing fighter:** Major + Auto Game Misconduct
- **Sucker puncher:** Major + Auto Game Misconduct (reviewable)
- **Off the ice:** Major + Auto Game Misconduct
- **Third person to intervene:** Major + Auto Game Misconduct

### High-sticking Penalties (Rule 60)
- **Accidental minor:** Minor (2 min)
- **Accidental with injury:** Double-Minor (4 min)
- **Contact to head with intent:** Major + Auto Game Misconduct (5 min)

### Video Review (Only in certain IIHF Championships)
- **Double-Minor High-stick:** Optional review (confirm or nullify)
- **Major Penalties:** Mandatory review (confirm, reduce, or nullify)
- **Fighting:** Only "Sucker puncher" can be reviewed

## üîÑ Penalty Clock Rules

### When Penalty Expires
- Minor/Bench Minor: Released on goal if short-handed (except coincidental)
- Double-Minor: One (1) min washes if goal scored during delay
- Major: Player STAYS in box (no early release on goal)
- Misconduct: Stays until NEXT stoppage after expiry

### Multiple Penalties Same Duration
- Captain designates which player returns first
- Ref instructs penalty timekeeper

### Face-Off After Penalty
- **Usual:** Offending team's end zone (team picks which spot)
- **Exception 1:** Center ice if goal was just scored
- **Exception 2:** Center ice if penalty at period start/end
- **Exception 3:** Neutral zone if defenders penalized and attackers too far in
